Breads

Our main breads are Brioche, Sourdough, Rye, Multigrain, Baguette, Sprouted, and Bagel.

Cakes

Our main cakes are Coffee, Fruits, Black Forest, Madeira, White Chocolate, and Festive.

Cookies

Our main cookies are Chocolate Chip, Shortbread, Macaroon, Biscotti, Oatmeal Raisin, Snickerdoodle, and Fortune.

Croissant
